📊 GET COACH FEEDBACK ↗
Non-negotiables
💪
5 workouts per week
Push · Cardio+Core · Pull · Legs · Run. Climbing/golf/hiking = bonus. Peloton enhances — never replaces (except Tue cardio).
🦵
PT every day — rotates so it's not the same
Mon/Thu: Wall sits + TKEs + Straight leg raises. Tue/Fri: Wall sits + Step-downs + Hip raises. Wed/Sat: Wall sits + Band step-down + Clamshells. Sun: Light mix.
🚴
Peloton app — how it works
Tue: Peloton HIIT ride IS your cardio. Fri: Peloton guides your run on your treadmill. All other days: optional add-on stretch, yoga, or core class.
⛳
Golf + hiking count as bonus activity
Log them in the Activity tab. Walking 18 holes = ~1,400 cal burned. A long hike counts as active recovery. Neither replaces your 5 workouts.
🍗
150g protein daily
1g per pound of body weight. Without this you lose muscle while cutting — opposite of what you want for the wedding.
😴
7+ hours sleep
This is when your body actually changes. Poor sleep spikes cortisol, stores belly fat, and kills knee recovery.
🍺
No alcohol Mon–Thu
Directly stalls fat loss and wrecks sleep. 1–2 drinks max on weekends.
🔙
Back protection always
No overhead barbell. No heavy barbell squats. Dumbbells + bodyweight + Peloton only. Sharp pain = stop immediately.
